Hi, I'm Tagnon Okoumassoun. I research on multi-robot autonomous systems. 

## What I'm working on
Multi-robot task allocation under capability uncertainty. Belief-space allocation where each robot's true capability is unknown and estimated probabilistically during execution.  

[**air-ground-ops**](https://github.com/privaelo/air-ground-ops.git) — ROS 2 / Gazebo sandbox with 1 UAV and 3 UGVs. Testbed for the allocation work above.  
[**modern_robotics**](https://github.com/privaelo/modern-robotics.git) — Notes from Kevin Lynch's Modern Robotics specialization.
